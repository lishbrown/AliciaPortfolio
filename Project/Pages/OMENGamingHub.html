OMEN Gaming Hub

One of my favorite projects I worked to create is the UX copy and user flow for the HP QuickDrop app.



We identified a common need among users to seamlessly transfer files, photos, videos, and more from their mobile devices to their PCs. From this information, a 3 person team created the HP QuickDrop app.



After collecting insights from the stakeholders, I mapped out common themes and helped create the user flow and craft the copy for the app.



The HP QuickDrop app has over 100k downloads and a 4.7 star rating on the Microsoft Store.
